What the world needs now is ... to find comfort in the unknown.

As a society, we're increasingly facing complex issues. Where better to look for advice than to a person who deals daily with the cosmic-sized conundrums? On the 8th of November 2020 astronomer David Gardenier from the University of Amsterdam will guide us on a journey to find the answers we're looking for..

NB. This talk will be in English
